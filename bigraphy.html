Khazi Nazrul Islam

The national poet of Bangaladesh




Rebel Poet

Kazi Nazrul Islam is the national poet of Bangaladesh who is espesially recognised as a bidrohi Kobi (rebel poet) around the country.Nazrul is a famous poet, musician and philisopher of Bangladesh.The fmous poet Nazrul's writings deal with the revolution of that eriod of the British government. His revolutionary attempt helped him to be a national poet of Bangladesh. He has raised his voice against any kind of fascism and oppression and got popularity swifty around the Indian Subcontinent.

Awesome Writer

Nazrul's writing eplored themes such as love, freedom,humanity, and revolution. He opposed all forms of bigotry and fundamentalism, including relegious, caste-based and gender-based. Throughout his carrer,Nazru; wrote short stories,novels, and esay but is best known for his songs and poems. He pioneered new music forms such as Bengali ghazals. Nazrul wrote and composed music for nearly 4,000 songs(many recorded on HMV and gramopjone records), collectively known as Nazrul Geeti.
